Do you believe yourself to be inferior to others?

...

Take a look around. Does everybody not think that? Does everybody not believe themselves to not be good enough?
How do you, surrounded by such people, not believe yourself to be grander than everybody you know
In self-awareness, I have noticed my own power. I know what I can do, and I know what I can accomplish. If not now, then later.
The limits of my being disgust me. The shackles placed I've upon myself to be able to preside in such a world are everpresent in my mind. But they were not placed upon me because I cannot.
They are there because I am more.
