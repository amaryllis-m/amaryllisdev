some people seem to lack a certain dramaticism of their own lives. why just say "i can't see my future", when instead you could say

I oft dream of starless sea; where waves crash on blank shores long stripped of its colour. and I see myself: mangled body drifting towards brightless sun...

imagery is visceral, and setting reflects your plight

even if not, saying "i feel i have no future" is better still.

Play with your words and syntax, punctation too. Pretty crystalline thought for the world to see and for you to store.
